Here's a time line of Kip Thorne's life:

  • 1940 - Born in Logan, Utah
  • 1962 - Received B.S. degree from Caltech
  • 1965 - Received Ph.D. degree from Princeton University
  • 1967 - Became an associate professor at Caltech
  • 1970 - Became a professor of theoretical physics at Caltech
  • 1972 - Elected into the American Academy of Arts and Sciences
  • 1973 - Co-authored textbook Gravitation with Charles Misner and John Wheeler
  • 1980 - Carl Sagan sets up blind date with Lynda Obst the science editor at the New York Times Magazine
  • 1981 - Became the William R. Kenan, Jr. Professor
  • 1984 - Cofounded LIGO (Laser Interferometer Gravitational Wave Observatory)
  • 1991 - Became the Feynman Pforessor of Theoretical Physics
  • 1994 - Published Black Holes and Time Warps: Einstein's Outrageous Legacy
  • 1996 - Received the German Astronomical Society's Karl Schwarzschild Medal
  • 2003 - Received the California Science Center's California Scientist of the Year Award
  • 2005 - Concocted the original treatment for "Interstellar"
  • 2006 - First meeting whith Steven Spielberg about "Interstellar"
  • 2007 - Jonathan Nolan, Lynda Obst, and Steven Spielberg agree on structure for "Interstellar"
  • 2009 - Became the Feynman Professor of Theoretical Physics, Emeritus
  • 2010 - Steven Spelberg backs out of the next phase of "Interstellar"
  • 2012 - Christopher Nolan becomes director for "Interstellar"
  • 2012 - Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Michael Caine, Jessica Chastain selected for roles in "Interstellar"
  • 2014 - Published The Science of Interstellar in which he explainsthe science behind Christopher Nolan's film Interstellar
  • 2015 - LIGO recorded the signature of two black holes colliding 1.3 billion light-years away
  • 2016 - Awarded the Special Breakthrough Prize in Fundamental Physics


"I do hope that 'Interstellar' and this kind of science in film will catch the public fancy and help to reignite an interest in science - and a respect for the power of science in dealing with the problems that society has to deal with."
